# Connectors

| Connector | Meaning | Example (FR) | Example (EN) | Added |
|-----------|---------|--------------|--------------|-------|
| donc | therefore/so | Il pleut, donc je reste à la maison. | It is raining, so I am staying home. | 2026-05-18 |
| parce que | because | Je suis en retard parce que j'ai raté le bus. | I am late because I missed the bus. | 2026-05-18 |
| cependant | however | Il fait froid, cependant je sors. | It is cold, however I am going out. | 2026-05-18 |
| alors | then | Il a fini son travail, alors il est parti. | He finished his work, then he left. | 2026-05-18 |
| puisque | since/because | Puisque tu es là, restons ensemble. | Since you are here, let's stay together. | 2026-05-18 |
